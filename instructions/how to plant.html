Step 1: Right Place, Right Plant - Start by arranging your plants so they're in a spot where they'll get the kind of light they prefer

Step 2: Dig the Soil - You don't need to dig a large area to plant, but you should dig enough soil that you can add some compost to improve the soil structure and add nutrients.

Step 3: Plant Your New plants - When removing the plant from the pot, gently tease some or all of the soil from the roots and place the plant into the hole you've prepared.

Step 4: Water Deeply and Add Mulch - It's best to water deeply and less frequently than shallowly and more often so the roots of the plants grow deeper.
